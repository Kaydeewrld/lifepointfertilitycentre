WE STRIVE TO MAKE YOUR DREAM OF PARENTHOOD A REALITY.

At Lifepoint Fertility Clinic, we understand the emotional and physical journey of trying to conceive. Our In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) program is designed to offer hope and personalized care to individuals and couples facing fertility challenges.

WHY CHOOSE IVF AT LIFEPOINT IVF CLINIC?

THE IVF PROCESS AT LIFEPOINT IVF CLINIC

Ovary Stimulation/ Egg Retrieval



You will receive daily injections of fertility hormones to stimulate egg production. Performed under sedation, a thin needle is used to collect eggs from the ovaries through the vaginal wall. This procedure is minimally invasive and usually takes about 20-30 minutes.

Fertilization



Depending on the specifics of your case, either conventional IVF (where eggs and sperm are mixed) or Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI, where a single sperm is injected directly into an egg) is used to fertilize the eggs.

Embryo Transfer



The selected embryo(s) are gently placed into the uterus using a thin catheter. This procedure is usually painless and doesn’t require anesthesia. Hormonal support is provided to help prepare the uterine lining for implantation.
